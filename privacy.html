1 Who We Are & Scope

Growziq is a web design and development service operating from Ludhiana, Punjab, India. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal data in connection with our services. By submitting our intake form and proceeding with our services, you consent to the practices described herein. This Policy operates in conjunction with our Terms of Service.

Growziq is a Data Fiduciary as defined under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act) to the extent applicable to our operations and scale. We process personal data only for the purpose of delivering services you have contracted with us.

3 How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively for the following purposes. We do not use your data for any other purpose without your explicit consent.

  • Delivering the web design and development service you have contracted - building, deploying, and handing over your website per the confirmed scope.
  • Communicating with you throughout the project - sending scope confirmations, invoices, progress updates, feedback requests, and handover documentation.
  • Generating and issuing invoices and maintaining financial records as required by Indian tax law.
  • Responding to support requests during the 7-day Support Period.
  • Maintaining records for dispute resolution - project scope, communications, and delivery documentation retained to defend against or resolve client disputes.
  • Complying with legal obligations - retaining financial records per the Income Tax Act, 1961 and responding to lawful requests from government authorities.

We do NOT use your data for: advertising, marketing to third parties, profiling, selling to third parties, or any purpose unrelated to delivering your project.

4 Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary for the stated purpose or as required by law.

Data Category Retention Period Reason
Project communications (email/WhatsApp) 24 months from project close Dispute resolution / Internal records
Intake form data 36 months from project close Dispute resolution / consent records
Invoices & payment records 7 years Income Tax Act, 1961 (mandatory)

Financial records exception: Even if you request erasure of your personal data, Growziq is legally required to retain invoices and payment records for 7 years under the Income Tax Act. Where erasure is requested, we will delete all data except legally mandated financial records and notify you in writing of what was retained and the legal basis.

WhatsApp note: WhatsApp (Meta) may retain message data on their servers per their own privacy policy, outside Growziq's control. Project data deletion by Growziq is "where technically feasible" - we cannot guarantee deletion from third-party servers.

6 Your Rights

As a Data Principal under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023, you have the following rights in respect of your personal data held by Growziq:

  • Right to Access: Obtain a summary of your personal data we hold and how it has been processed.
  • Right to Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal retention obligations.
  • Right to Grievance Redressal: File a complaint with Growziq's Grievance Officer.
  • Right to Nominate: Nominate another individual to exercise these rights on your behalf in the event of your death or incapacity.

To exercise any of the above rights, email dev.growziq@gmail.com with the subject line "Data Rights Request - [Your Name]". We will verify your identity before processing the request to prevent fraudulent data access or deletion.

7 Consent

  • Consent to processing your personal data is obtained at the point of intake form submission via a mandatory, opt-in checkbox.
  • The consent checkbox records: (a) your agreement to this Privacy Policy, (b) your agreement to the Terms of Service, (c) your confirmation that you are above 18 years of age and authorized to represent your business.
  • Consent metadata recorded includes: submission timestamp, IP address, and form version. These records are retained for 36 months as evidence of valid consent.
  • Consent is freely given, specific, informed, and unambiguous as required under the DPDP Act, 2023. The checkbox is not pre-ticked.
  • You may withdraw consent at any time by contacting dev.growziq@gmail.com. Withdrawal does not apply retroactively to processing already performed under valid consent.

8 Security & Breach Response

Growziq implements reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, loss, alteration, or disclosure. Specific measures include:

  • Access to project data restricted to the two Growziq partners only - no third-party employees or contractors with data access.
  • Client hosting credentials stored temporarily and deleted immediately upon handover.
  • Project communications archived on password-protected accounts.
  • Payment processing delegated entirely to Razorpay - Growziq never receives or stores card or banking credentials.

In the event of a personal data breach likely to result in harm to you, Growziq will notify you at your registered email address as soon as reasonably practicable and comply with applicable notification obligations under the DPDP Act.

No method of data transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we implement best-effort security measures, Growziq cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

9 Cookies

  • Growziq's website uses only essential cookies required for basic website functionality.
  • No advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or behavioral profiling tools are used without your explicit consent.
  • Google Analytics may be present for basic traffic analytics. If so, a cookie consent mechanism will be in place. Google Analytics data is anonymous and aggregated - it does not identify you personally.
  • The Tally intake form embedded on the site may set functional cookies required for form operation. These are governed by Tally's cookie policy.

10 Minors

Growziq's services are intended exclusively for persons above 18 years of age or business entities. We do not knowingly collect personal data from individuals below 18 years of age. The intake form requires confirmation that the submitter is above 18. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected data from a person below 18, we will delete it immediately.

11 Cross-Border Transfers

Some of our third-party processors (Tally, Zapier, Google, Meta/WhatsApp) may store or process data on servers outside India. By consenting to this Privacy Policy, you acknowledge and consent to such transfers to the extent they occur in connection with delivering your project. Growziq itself operates from India and does not intentionally transfer your data outside India beyond what is incidental to our use of the above third-party processors.

12 Policy Updates

  • Growziq may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be notified to you via email at your registered address with at least 30 days advance notice.
  • The updated Policy will be published at growziq.in/privacy with a revised effective date.
  • Continued engagement with Growziq's services after the effective date of an updated Policy constitutes acceptance of the revised terms.
  • Changes do not apply retroactively to data already collected and processed under a prior version of this Policy.

13 Governing Law & Disputes

This Privacy Policy is governed by the laws of India, including the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023, the Information Technology Act, 2000, the IT (Reasonable Security Practices) Rules, 2011, and the Indian Contract Act, 1872.

Any disputes arising from this Privacy Policy shall be resolved in accordance with the dispute resolution mechanism set out in Growziq's Terms of Service - by arbitration in Ludhiana, Punjab.
