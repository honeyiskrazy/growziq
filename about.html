We got tired of watching good businesses stay invisible online.

Local businesses shouldn't need a massive budget or a computer science degree to have a professional website.

We started Growziq because we noticed a massive gap in the market. On one side, you have expensive agencies quoting ₹20,000+ for basic landing pages and trapping owners in monthly "maintenance" retainers. On the other side, you have DIY builders like Wix or WordPress that claim to be easy, but end up taking weeks to configure and often look unpolished.

Most small business owners in India don't want a complicated tech project. They just want a digital storefront that looks trustworthy, loads fast, and tells customers what they do.

So we built a fixed-price solution. No corporate jargon. No hidden fees. We build your site, hand over the keys, and let you get back to running your business.
