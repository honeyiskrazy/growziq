Frequently Asked Questions

Do I fully own the website after paying once?
Yes. Once your project is delivered and you confirm it, all logins, files, and access are transferred to you completely. We don't hold anything back. No monthly fees, no renewal charges to us, no dependency on Growziq to keep your site running. It's yours — permanently.
Do I have to pay separately for hosting and domain?
Your domain and hosting are purchased by you directly — not through us. We recommend free hosting options wherever possible, so in many cases your only extra cost is the domain name. We handle the full setup for you. The reason we do it this way is so you own everything — no agency can ever hold your website hostage.
What if I don't like the website — will I get a refund?
We confirm your exact scope in writing before we start — exactly what will be built, how many pages, what features. You approve it before paying. This means by the time we deliver, you already know what to expect. If something from the confirmed scope is missing or broken, we fix it free within 7 days. Refunds are not available once work has started, which is why we lock the scope in writing first — so there are no surprises.
How long will the website take?
Work starts from the day we receive your content — your logo, photos, text, and hosting access. From that point, typical delivery is 7-30 working days depending on your plan. The timeline is in your hands too — the faster you send content and give feedback, the faster we deliver. We keep you updated throughout.
Will SEO bring my business on Google and get me customers?
We set up all the technical on-page SEO — meta tags, sitemap, schema, Google Search Console, Analytics (based on your plan). This gives Google everything it needs to find and understand your website. What we cannot guarantee is your ranking position or how many customers will come — that depends on Google's algorithm, your competition, and your content quality. Anyone who guarantees you Page 1 rankings is lying to you.
What if I need changes later?
Every plan includes a 7-day support window after delivery — we fix anything that's broken or missing from what was agreed. After those 7 days, additional changes are available as paid add-ons at straightforward per-change rates. Premium plan clients also get 5 content edits free within 30 days of delivery. We keep it simple — no mystery pricing, you know the rate before we start.
Do you only work with local businesses?
We work with businesses across India — fully remote. All communication happens over WhatsApp and email, so location doesn't matter. We've worked with businesses in different cities. The only thing we ask is that you're reachable and responsive during the project — that's what keeps the timeline on track.
